What We Offer

Our Services

A comprehensive range of psychological services tailored to the needs of individuals, attorneys, and fellow healthcare professionals.

Diagnostic Assessments

Comprehensive clinical evaluations to clarify diagnoses, inform treatment planning, and provide a clear understanding of psychological functioning across a range of conditions.

Psychometric Testing

Standardized, validated testing instruments administered and interpreted by a licensed professional to quantify cognitive, emotional, and behavioral functioning with precision.

Record Review

Careful and systematic examination of medical, psychiatric, educational, and legal records to synthesize history, identify patterns, and support clinical or forensic opinions.

Independent Medical Examinations

Objective, impartial psychological evaluations conducted for legal, insurance, or administrative proceedings. Thorough assessments grounded in professional standards and ethical practice.

Psychoeducation

Structured education for patients and families about psychological conditions, treatment options, and coping strategies — empowering informed engagement with the therapeutic process.

Professional Consultation

Collegial consultation for attorneys, clinicians, and organizations seeking expert guidance on case conceptualization, diagnostic questions, or treatment direction.

Our Approach

Rigorous, Compassionate, Clear

At NMB Psychological Services, every evaluation is approached with the same commitment: thoroughness in method, warmth in manner, and clarity in communication. We believe that a well-conducted assessment can be the foundation for meaningful progress — whether for a patient seeking understanding, an attorney building a case, or a clinician refining a treatment plan. Our work is grounded in current research, guided by ethical standards, and delivered with the care each individual deserves.
